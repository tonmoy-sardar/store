The PII we may get from you in that situation are strictly limited to your name, email address ,contact number and your survey response only.

In above situation, we guarantee that your PII will only be used for contacting you and improving our services. It should be noted that whether or not to send us your feedback or bug report is a completely voluntary initiative upon your own decision. If you have concern about your PII being misused, or if you want further information about our privacy policy and what it means, please feel free to email us at support@banaoapp.com, we will endeavor to provide clear answers to your questions in a timely manner.
